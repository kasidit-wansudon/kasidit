# Code of Conduct

Kasidit follows the **Contributor Covenant v2.1**. The goal is a productive, technical community where contributors of any background can participate.

## In short

- Be respectful and direct.
- Critique code, not people.
- Disagree without disparaging.
- Assume good faith; ask before accusing.
- Keep discussion technical and on-topic.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, the wiki, and any official communication channel — and to any individual representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainer at **kasidit.wans@gmail.com**.

Reports are kept confidential. The maintainer will respond, investigate proportionally, and take action — which may include warnings, edits, locked threads, removals, or bans depending on severity and pattern.
